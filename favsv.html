Category Activity Reason I Like It
Farming Walking around my farm and seeing the automation I painstakingly progressed to. I remember starting with a regular watering pail. Now I have automation so I don't have to worry about begrudgingly watering each square.
Optimizing Crafting little improvements for the farm. At this point in my game, there's not any major jumps to be made. I love this because now I can focus on little improvements to better optimize my farm and output. I love finding ways to improve and get better at producing.
Story/Characters Walking around talking to the villagers, watching cutscenes, etc. Every character has such a unique set of dialogue and side story. I love them all and want to see their subtle improvements.
